Delete Your Data

Minedive

You can request deletion of your Minedive account and associated data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to mysticgames.play@gmail.com
  2. Include "Delete My Data" in the subject line
  3. Include your in-game nickname so we can locate your data
Request Data Deletion

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your leaderboard scores
  • Your nickname and nickname claim
  • Your cloud sync data (game progress, unlocks)
  • Any data associated with your Play Games ID

Data Retention

Once we receive your request, your data will be deleted within 30 days. Local data stored on your device (preferences, cached scores) can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

What We Cannot Delete

Anonymous analytics data that has already been aggregated cannot be individually deleted as it is not linked to your identity.
