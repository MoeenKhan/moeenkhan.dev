Privacy Policy

Minedive

This privacy policy explains how Minedive ("the game", "we", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you play the game.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Google Play Games ID - Used to identify your account for cloud sync and leaderboards (Android only)
  • Display Name - Your Google Play Games player name (Android only)
  • Device ID - A randomly generated identifier stored locally, used to limit daily challenge attempts
  • Nickname - A custom in-game name you choose for the leaderboard
  • Game Data - Your scores, unlocked features, and gameplay progress
  • Analytics Data - Anonymous gameplay events such as floors reached, items used, and game completion (no personal information)

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Display your scores on public leaderboards
  • Sync your game progress across devices (Android with Play Games)
  • Ensure fair play in daily challenges (one attempt per player)
  • Improve the game through anonymous analytics

Third-Party Services

The game uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Games Services - For sign-in and player identification on Android. See Google's Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Realtime Database - For storing leaderboard data and game progress. See Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics - For anonymous gameplay analytics to help improve the game. See Google's Privacy Policy

Data Storage

Your game data is stored in Firebase servers. Local game preferences are stored on your device using standard browser/app storage.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your nickname and scores are publicly visible on leaderboards.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you use the game. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

The game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to request data deletion, contact us at: mysticgames.play@gmail.com
